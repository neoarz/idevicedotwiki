description: Guides for understanding how idevice connects to devices and services.
---

The guides explain the parts of `idevice` that are hard to learn from API
reference alone.

- [Architecture](/docs/guides/architecture): the stack from usbmuxd to services.
- [Pairing](/docs/guides/pairing): local pairing and remote pairing.
- [iOS 17+ Tunnels](/docs/guides/ios-17-tunnels): RSD, RemoteXPC, and CoreDevice tunnels.
- [CoreDevice Services](/docs/guides/coredevice-services): newer services like HID, pasteboard, and screen capture.
- [File & App Services](/docs/guides/file-app-services): AFC, app containers, app install, and developer images.
- [Developer Services](/docs/guides/developer-services): DVT, XCTest, WDA, and related tooling.
- [Debugging & Logs](/docs/guides/debugging-logs): syslog, OS trace, debug proxy, PCAP, crash logs, and Bluetooth capture.
- [Backup & Restore](/docs/guides/backup-restore): MobileBackup2, restore services, and TSS.
