---
title: iOS 17+ Tunnels
description: RemoteXPC, RSD, and CoreDevice tunnel concepts.
---

iOS 17+ moved many developer services away from the older lockdown service
path.

The newer path uses:

- RSD for service discovery.
- RemoteXPC for XPC messages over HTTP/2.
- CoreDevice tunnel services for protected developer features.
- Remote pairing records for trusted hosts.

The tunnel is the bridge between the device and newer protected services. Once
the path is established, CoreDevice and DVT-style clients can connect through
RSD instead of asking lockdown for a service port.

Relevant features:

- `rsd`
- `xpc`
- `remote_pairing`
- `core_device`
- `core_device_proxy`
- `tunnel_tcp_stack`
- `tunneld`

Useful CLI commands:

```bash
idevice-tools remotexpc
idevice-tools rppairing
idevice-tools screencapture
idevice-tools hid
```
